About

This is my story.

  • Mid 2014

    The First Thoughts

    I used to be a commercial/industrial Electrician and project manager in the Electrical contracting business. In 2014, I started thinking about making some changes in my life, I wanted a new career.

  • Early 2015

    The Coding Journey Begins

    I've always computers, the internet and technology in general. With the influence of a few friends, I began the journey of learning how to code. At this point I started taking courses with a few online schools like, Treehouse, CodeSchool and FreeCodeCamp.

  • March 2017

    Transition to IT

    In March 2017, I got an opportunity to work for Lennar Homes in their Aliso Viejo IT department. That was a good first step into the IT world. In the meantime, I kept learning web development on the side.

  • September 2019

    Another Level

    As the right time and the right school came around, I decided to join the UCI Coding Bootcamp full time. The future is bright.
